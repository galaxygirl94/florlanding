Welcome to Flor

Our Mission

Nursing is one of the most important professions in the world, yet nurses are constantly battling exploitative staffing agencies, opaque pay practices, and endless recruiter spam. We're changing that.

Flor eliminates the middleman by connecting nurses directly with healthcare facilities. No more hidden fees eating into your paycheck. No more bait-and-switch job postings. No more agencies taking 40% markups while you do all the work.

We're building a platform where transparency isn't optional—it's required. Where facilities compete for you, not the other way around. Where you have the power to make informed decisions about your career.

What Makes Flor Different

💰 Transparent Pay, Always

Every job listing shows the real salary range upfront. No surprises, no "competitive pay" nonsense.

🚫 Zero Recruiter Spam

Apply directly to facilities. No middlemen calling and texting you constantly with irrelevant positions.

✨ Honest Job Descriptions

Facilities sign an ethics pledge to provide accurate staffing ratios, shift details, and working conditions.

🎯 Built by Nurses

Our co-founder is a registered nurse with 8 years of experience. We understand what you need because we've lived it.

💪 Free for Nurses, Forever

You'll never pay a dime to use Flor. Facilities pay to post jobs—not you.
